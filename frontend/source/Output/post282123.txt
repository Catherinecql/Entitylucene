/code/pre  <answer282129> Apple doesn't configure a tty for USB ports without a connected adapter. It's up to the hardware you connect to either have drivers or expose the drivers Apple has shipped.   The venerable Keyspan USA-19HS has been my weapon of choice for serial to USB needs since before this web site was born.
